What's OdinAI all about?
OdinAI is here to help. We help you get better at Deep Learning and connect you with opportunities. Our content is divided into two major themes:

#1
Deep Learning how to’s and much more.

#2
Content created by college graduates and seniors who are currently either working in Deep Learning or pursuing research (or just involved in DL) / who currently work in Deep Learning, detailing methods to efficiently learn Deep Learning at particular colleges (and making the best use of a college’s resources). Details on what courses to take, clubs to join, what professors to work under, and all the other tid-bits only a college graduate knows. Essentially, a Deep Learning survival guides for different schools.
Contributers
Made with ♥ for you by students and alumni from various universities, NIT Trichy, IIT Madras, PICT -- and counting
